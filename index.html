What Exactly Is BrigidFlow?

BrigidFlow is a lightweight, AI‑powered planning dashboard that turns biometric and cycle data from your wearable into practical work decisions:

  • Forecast Capacity – See a 14‑day energy forecast based on hormone phase and sleep quality.
  • Scope With Confidence – Our quote plug‑in auto‑adds buffer time when your capacity dips, so you never miss a deadline.
  • Plan Your Week – Colour‑coded Google Calendar overlay highlights deep‑work windows and recovery blocks.
  • Batch & Automate – One‑click Zapier actions batch content during high‑energy days and schedule drip release.
  • Respect Privacy – Only anonymised capacity scores leave your device; raw health data stays encrypted.

Think of it as your personal project manager that speaks hormone. No matter if your cycle runs like clockwork or you’re navigating PCOS, endometriosis, or postpartum shifts—BrigidFlow translates body signals into sustainable productivity.
